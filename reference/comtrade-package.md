# comtrade: Access and Analyse UN Comtrade International Trade Data

Download and analyse international merchandise and services trade data
from the United Nations Comtrade database
<https://comtradeplus.un.org/>. Retrieve bilateral trade flows, compute
trade analytics (revealed comparative advantage, trade concentration,
trade balance), and convert between commodity classifications
(Harmonised System 'HS', Standard International Trade Classification
'SITC', Broad Economic Categories 'BEC'). Covers 200+ reporter
countries, 60+ years of goods trade data (1962-present), and services
trade via Extended Balance of Payments Services ('EBOPS'). Works without
registration for basic queries. A free Application Programming Interface
('API') key from <https://comtradedeveloper.un.org/> unlocks full
access.
